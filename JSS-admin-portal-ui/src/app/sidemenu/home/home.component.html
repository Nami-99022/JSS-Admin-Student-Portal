About JSS Polytechnic

The JSS Polytechnic, Mysuru was established in the year 1986. The campus is situated on an extensive elevated stretch of over 6 Hectares in the JSS Technical Institutions’ Campus to the west of the University of Mysuru, Manasagangothri..

Our Vision

Our vision is to foster an environment of academic excellence and innovation, empowering students to achieve their full potential and contribute to the global community.
